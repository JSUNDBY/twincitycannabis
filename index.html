Questions from dispensary owners

Can you just buy a higher ranking?
No. TCC Scores are algorithmic and never influenced by payment. Paid tiers get additional visibility in clearly-labeled "Featured" sections, but your organic search position is based on reviews, pricing, and selection.
Where does the product data come from?
We pull your full menu from Weedmaps automatically, updated multiple times daily. If you update your menu on Weedmaps, it appears on TCC within hours. You don't need to maintain anything separately.
What's the 30-day free trial?
Your Featured listing is active for 30 days with no payment. You'll see your analytics, your badge, and your placement in the Featured carousel. After 30 days, you decide if the traffic is worth $299/mo.
How is this different from Weedmaps?
Weedmaps is a national directory. TCC is a local price comparison tool built specifically for the Twin Cities. We help shoppers find the best price across YOUR local competitors, and we send that traffic directly to you. Our Featured tier is $299/mo vs Weedmaps' $400-1,500/mo.
What behavioral data do I get with Premium?
You'll see exactly how shoppers interact with your products vs competitors: how many people viewed your product, how many compared prices, what percentage chose you, and which products are driving the most traffic. This is data you can't get anywhere else.
