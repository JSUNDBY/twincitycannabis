What We Do

Twin City Cannabis is the only real-time price comparison site covering every cannabis dispensary in Minnesota. We pull your full menu multiple times a day. Consumers use us to compare prices, find the nearest dispensary, and read reviews before they choose where to shop.

Your dispensary is already listed for free. Menu, hours, address, Google reviews, and TCC Score are all live right now.

Real example: The Grasslandz Durban Poison 1g cart is $48 at one metro store and $70 at another. Shoppers see this comparison on TCC in seconds. If you're the $48 store, you want people to find you. If you're the $70 store, you need to understand why shoppers choose elsewhere.
